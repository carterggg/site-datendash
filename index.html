DATE-N-DASH'S FOCUS:

Our focus is being fast paced, sociable, and the best of all, DATE-N-DASH will have various speed dating and mixers events. "Why not try one of our upcoming events?" Our clients will be there to meet new people, expand social circles, and possibly meet someone special. Our events will allow you to mix and mingle with our exquisite guest list at our exclusive venue with a fun filled & exciting atmosphere with the focus on privacy and safety

GET STARTED:

(A) You will register online (you will receive email confirmation with details of event and if there are any changes.)


(B) Why not get your evening to a great start? We ask that you arrive 15-30 minutes before scheduled time as we aim to punctual start all our events.


(C) Next your host will welcome you as a group & explain how everything works! You will be provided with a name badge, Date-N-Dash Questionaire Card, and a pen.


(D) Lights will be dimmed, music goes up, and the excitement begins!



DURING:

You will have face to face dates with other single people (sounds fun already), the Date-N-Dash timers will come out and you will have 6-8 minutes to ask questions according to DATE-N-DASH Questionnaire Cards!

AFTER:

Afterwards you can enjoy music, mingle, or DATE OR DASH!
